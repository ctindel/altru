Celebrate Earth Day with us at our annual Eco-Festival featuring live music, workshops on sustainable living, native plant sales, and interactive exhibits about renewable energy. A fun, family-friendly event that connects people with nature.

## Festival Highlights

Enjoy workshops on composting, solar energy, and organic gardening. Meet local conservation groups, sample sustainable food vendors, and participate in our signature tree planting ceremony.
