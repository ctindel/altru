## Welcome to Altru Environmental Organization

Altru is a leading environmental nonprofit dedicated to conservation, reforestation, and building a sustainable future. We work with communities, scientists, and policymakers to address the most pressing environmental challenges of our time.

From planting millions of trees to protecting critical wildlife habitats, our programs create lasting change. We believe that everyone has a role to play in safeguarding the natural world, and we provide the tools, resources, and opportunities to make a real difference.
