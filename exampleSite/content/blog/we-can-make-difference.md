Making a difference for the planet does not require a complete lifestyle overhaul. Small, consistent changes in daily habits can significantly reduce your environmental impact. From choosing public transit to reducing food waste, every action counts.

## Start Where You Are

The most impactful step you can take is often the simplest one. Begin by evaluating your energy use at home, reconsidering your transportation habits, and being more intentional about what you consume. Collective small actions lead to transformative change.
